# User Stories, Personas, and Value Propositions for the Connexted Platform

## User Personas

### Innovators
- **Background**: Individuals or teams looking to bring a new idea or product to market.
- **Goals**: To find collaborators, gain feedback on prototypes, and increase visibility of their projects.
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty in finding the right people with complementary skills; lack of access to networks.

### Startups
- **Background**: Newly established businesses seeking to grow and network.
- **Goals**: To connect with other startups, potential investors, and customers; to share successes and challenges.
- **Pain Points**: Limited resources and connection; difficulty in standing out in a crowded market.

### Career Transitioners
- **Background**: Individuals looking to change their career path or learn new skills.
- **Goals**: To connect with mentors, gain new skills, and discover job opportunities.
- **Pain Points**: Uncertainty about the next steps; lack of clear pathways to new careers.

## User Stories

### As an Innovator:
1. **I want to find collaborators easily**, so I can enhance my project with different skills.
2. **I want to receive constructive feedback on my ideas**, so I can improve them before launch.
3. **I want to showcase my work on a platform**, so potential investors and users can discover my projects.

### As a Startup:
1. **I want to connect with other startups**, to share resources and learn from each other.
2. **I want to gain visibility among potential investors**, so I can secure funding for my growth.
3. **I want to join circles of similar businesses**, so I can network in a more focused manner.

### As a Career Transitioner:
1. **I want to find mentors in my desired field**, to guide me through the transition.
2. **I want to access resources for learning new skills**, like workshops or online courses.
3. **I want to engage with a community of like-minded individuals**, to share experiences and support each other.

## Value Propositions
- **Community Building**: The platform fosters a sense of belonging and community for users across different backgrounds.
- **Increased Discoverability**: Users and their projects gain visibility, which is critical for success in their respective fields.
- **Collaborative Opportunities**: Through circles and community engagement, users can find collaboration opportunities that enhance their work.
- **Ownership of Development**: Users have the freedom to choose pathways that best suit their professional journey and personal development.
- **Cohort Programs**: Structured learning opportunities are available, providing support and resources as users transition into new careers or ventures.
