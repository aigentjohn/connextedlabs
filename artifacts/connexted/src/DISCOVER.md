# Discover — Feature Guide

The **Discover** section helps you find content in four different ways: by browsing what exists, by tracking topics and tags you care about, by following people whose work matters to you, and by surfacing what the community values most.

## Explore Containers

**Path:** Discover → Explore Containers

Browse and join the collaborative spaces on the platform — Circles, Programs, Tables, Meetups, Standups, Sprints, and more.

- Filter by container type using the type selector
- Search by name or description
- Sort by newest, oldest, or most liked
- Click **Join** on open containers to become a member immediately
- Click **Request Access** on closed containers to submit a membership request

> Containers are collaborative workspaces. They hold content, host members, and have their own activity feeds.

## Browse Content

**Path:** Discover → Browse Content

Browse individual content items across the platform by type. Unlike containers, these are standalone pieces of content published by members.

**Available content types:**

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Episodes | Audio or video episodes from member channels |
| Books | Book recommendations and summaries shared by members |
| Blogs | Blog posts and articles |
| Playlists | Curated episode collections |
| Documents | Reference documents, guides, and resources |
| Courses | Self-paced learning modules |
| Builds | Projects and builds in progress or completed |
| Pitches | Business pitches and ideas |
| Decks | Slide decks and presentations |
| Magazines | Curated magazine-style collections |

**Sort options** within each type:
- **Newest** — most recently created first
- **Oldest** — earliest first
- **Most Liked** — ranked by community likes

Use the search bar within each tab to filter by title or description.

## Topics

**Path:** Discover → Topics

Topics are the structured subject taxonomy of the platform — curated categories that organise content by **who it's for** and **why they need it**.

### Topic types

- **Audience** — who the content is aimed at (e.g. Early-Stage Founders, Product Managers)
- **Purpose** — what the content helps with (e.g. Fundraising, Hiring, Go-to-Market)
- **Theme** — subject areas (e.g. AI, Sustainability, Community Building)

### Watching a topic

Click the **🔔 Bell** icon on any topic card or topic detail page to watch it.

- Watched topics feed content into your [Following Feed](#following-feed)
- Any content linked to that topic by platform admins will appear in your feed
- The bell turns indigo when active

To stop watching, click the bell again.

### Topic detail page

Each topic has a dedicated page showing:
- All content linked to that topic, browsable by type
- Watcher count — how many members are watching this topic
- A subscribe/unwatch button in the header

## Tags

**Path:** Discover → Tags

Tags are free-text labels that members add to their content. Unlike topics (which are curated), tags emerge organically from what members create.

### Tag types

- **WHAT tags** — describe the subject matter of the content
- **HOW tags** — describe the format or delivery method

### Following a tag

Click the **🔔 Bell** icon on any tag card to follow it.

- Followed tags feed content into your [Following Feed](#following-feed)
- Any content using that tag will surface in your feed
- The bell turns indigo when active

### Tag detail page

Each tag's detail page shows all content using that tag, filterable by content type (posts, documents, episodes, courses, and more).

## Rankings

**Path:** Discover → Rankings

See what the community is using and watching most.

### Top Tags tab

Tags ranked by how many times they appear across all content on the platform. Each tag shows a breakdown by content type (documents, episodes, threads, etc.).

Click any tag to go to its detail page.

### Top Topics tab

Topics ranked with two sort options:

| Sort | Ranks by |
|---|---|
| **By Content** | Number of content items linked to the topic |
| **By Watchers** | Number of members watching the topic |

Switch between the two using the toggle in the card header. The bold stat on each row reflects the active sort. Both counts are always visible.

## Following Feed

**Path:** Discover → Following Feed

A unified activity feed combining content from three sources you control:

- **People you follow** — recent posts, threads, documents, builds, pitches, events, and reviews from members you follow
- **Topics you watch** — content linked to any topic you have watched
- **Tags you follow** — content tagged with any tag you follow

Each item shows a source badge indicating why it appeared:
- No badge — from someone you follow directly
- **📌 Topic name** — surfaced via a topic you watch
- **🏷️ #tag** — surfaced via a tag you follow

Items are deduplicated — if a piece of content matches multiple sources, it appears once.

Use the search bar to filter by title, description, or author name. Click **Refresh** to reload the latest activity.

## Followers Feed

**Path:** Discover → Followers Feed

Shows recent content published by the members who follow **you** — a way to stay aware of what your audience is creating.

Covers: posts, threads, documents, builds, pitches, events, and reviews.

> This is distinct from the Following Feed. Following Feed = content from people *you* follow. Followers Feed = content from people who follow *you*.

---

## Friends Feed

**Path:** Discover → Friends Feed

Shows content from your **mutual connections** — members where both of you follow each other.

A friends avatar strip at the top of the page shows who your current mutual connections are. Click any avatar to visit their profile.

If you have no mutual connections yet, the page suggests browsing members or visiting your friends list.

## Most Liked

**Path:** Discover → Most Liked

Surfaces the highest-liked content across the entire platform, ranked by community likes (❤️).

### Time range filter

| Filter | Shows |
|---|---|
| This Week | Content liked in the last 7 days |
| This Month | Content liked in the last 30 days (default) |
| This Year | Content liked in the last 365 days |
| All Time | All likes ever recorded |

### Type filter

After results load, pill buttons appear for each content type present in the results. Click a type to narrow to just that type. Click **All types** to see everything again.

Ranks are shown with medals for positions 1–3 (🥇🥈🥉) and numbered ranks (#4, #5…) for the rest.

## My Favorites

**Path:** Discover → My Favorites

All content and containers you have **starred** (⭐) anywhere on the platform — your personal saved collection.

Items are grouped by type (Content and Containers) and can be filtered using the type pills at the top.

To unfavorite an item, click the ✕ button on its card directly from this page — no need to navigate back to the original item.

> **Star vs Bell vs Heart** — three distinct actions:
> - ⭐ **Star** = Favorite (save for later, appears in My Favorites)
> - 🔔 **Bell** = Watch/Follow (subscribe, content flows into Following Feed)
> - ❤️ **Heart** = Like (endorse, contributes to Most Liked rankings)
